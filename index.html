About Us

Founded in 2020, Papa Juans began with a simple mission: to create delicious, authentic pizza that brings people together. Our chefs trained in Naples, Italy, to master the art of traditional pizza making before bringing those techniques home.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our dough is made fresh daily, and our signature sauce is a closely guarded family recipe that's been passed down through generations.

At Papa Juans, we're more than just a pizza place - we're a community hub where families, friends, and neighbors can gather to enjoy great food in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
